## Current Status: Request for Qualifications (RFQ)

SEAL Certifications is currently in the process of establishing our certified auditor partner program. We are actively seeking qualified auditing firms to become authorized certification issuers.

### Timeline

- **Now - December 31st, 2025**: RFC Phase & Auditor RFQ Period
- **Q1 2026**: Begin issuing formal certifications with certified auditor partners

## Becoming a Certified Auditor

SEAL will work with a select group of third-party auditing firms to provide certification audits. SEAL-certified auditors will demonstrate expertise in blockchain security and operational security practices, and will be authorized to conduct audits against the SEAL Certification Framework and issue on-chain attestations.

If your firm is interested, please fill [out this form](https://securityalliance.typeform.com/CertsAuditor)
